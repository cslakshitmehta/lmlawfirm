Lakshit Mehta Law Professional Corporation

Lakshit Mehta is a legal professional based in Ontario who founded Lakshit Mehta Law Professional Corporation. He graduated with Bachelors of Arts (Honours) in Mathematics from the University of Delhi, India and also did LL.B. from Lloyd Law College in India. . He is also a Chartered Secretary and an active member of the Institute of Company Secretaries of India (Chartered Secretary). Being an internationally trained lawyer, Lakshit has gained considerable experience in legal drafting, negotiations, litigations, legal research, corporate law compliance and managing regulatory affairs while working with various reputed organizations in India.

Lakshit was resolute in pursuing his further education in Canada where he completed his certification from the National Committee on Accreditation (NCA), Federation of Law Societies of Canada (FLSC). Post-certification, he appeared for the Barrister and Solicitor examinations of the Law Society of Ontario (LSO) and was called to Ontario Bar.

Lakshit Mehta Law Professional Corporation specializes in various fields of law, including Real Estate, Immigration Law, Civil Litigation, Wills & Power of Attorneys. The firm is committed to delivering the highest quality legal services to clients with efficiency and precision. The firm also takes pride in treating clients with courtesy, sensitivity, and confidentiality. By combining knowledge, skill, confidence, and personal care, we strive to produce the best possible & cost effective legal solution for our clients.

Our firm recognizes that English may not be the client’s preferred language, which is why we provide service in other languages, including Hindi and Punjabi.

Our firm’s services are available in the Greater Toronto Area and are available 24×7 for emergencies.

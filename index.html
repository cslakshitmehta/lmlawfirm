Lakshit Mehta is a legal professional based in Ontario who founded Lakshit Mehta Law Professional Corporation. He graduated with Bachelors of Arts (Honours) in Mathematics from the University of Delhi, India and also did LL.B. from Lloyd Law College in India. . He is also a Chartered Secretary and an active member of the Institute of Company Secretaries of India (Chartered Secretary).
